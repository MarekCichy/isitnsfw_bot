#myfirstbot
A simple Tweepy-powered bot that rates the NSFWness of photos tweeted by other users.
Using: [Clarifai](https://www.clarifai.com), [DeepAI](https://deepai.org) and [Sightengine](https://sightengine.com) APIs.
